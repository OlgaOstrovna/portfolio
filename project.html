Internally displaced persons

December 2017

×

When I was a Bachelor student of Taras Shevchenko National University in Ukraine, I have been working on the topic of migration and refugees and their problems in Ukraine during the period of the 2014-2018 year. In order to conduct analysis, I have created some basic maps to show the distribution of internally displaced persons from Donetsk, Luhansk regions and Autonomous Republic of Crimea during the period of March 2017. By the way, this map is one among my first maps.

Global Geospatial Conferences

January 2019

×

During my first semester of International Mater’s programme in Cartography, I have been working on creation of a web map representing information for easy navigation of all the conferences in Cartography & GIS all over the world. There was no single convenient resource containing the full information of all events and conferences in the world in the field of Cartography and GIS. So I got the motivation to create a convenient resource in order to help boost the exchange of knowledge by providing a single platform for information. You are very welcome to explore the map! I hope you will find some interesting carto events for yourself :)
